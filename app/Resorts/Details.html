About Hotel VITS

CHECK-IN 2 PM | CHECK-OUT 12 PM
Location: Located close to the Mumbai International Airport in Andheri East, VITS Mumbai Hotel is known for its plush interiors and quality service. Thus luxury destination for business travelers is located close to modes of connectivity such as JB Nagar Bus Station (300 m), Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport (2km) and Andheri Railway Station (3 Km). Major tourist attractions near the location are Siddhivinayak Temple (14.5 Km), Elephanta Caves (30 Km) and Gateway of India (30 Km).
Room Amenities: The rooms of Hotel VITS are elegantly furnished and tastefully designed in warm color tones for a luxurious stay. These rooms are classified into Standard Rooms, Superior Rooms and Suite Room/OBA. Basic room amenities offered by the hotel are Wi-Fi, hot water, air conditioning, Television, complimentary breakfast, tea/coffee maker, in-room safe, wardrobe, and bathrobe and bathroom slippers.
Hotel Facilities: There is Ayur Kshetra Spa at Hotel VITS which offers Ayurvedic, Swedish, Aroma and other therapies along with beauty treatments. Other prominent hotel facilities include a large swimming pool, well equipped fitness centre, travel desk and well equipped business centre. Hotel VITS has a total of 10 banquet halls for holding meetings and private functions. These are classified into Topaz Hall, Coral Hall, Ruby Hall, Onyx Hall (1, 2, 3, 4), Emerald Hall, Sapphire Hall, swimming pool, fitness centre, Crystal hall and the Rooftop area.
Dining: Poolside cafe, a Chinese restaurant and a bar delivers food and drinks of the highest standard. This is one of the perfect places in the hotel to relax and enjoy refreshing beverages along with host of multi-cuisine dishes and it stays open 24 X 7. Dimsum, the hotel restaurant serves delectable dishes of Chinese cuisine. Q Bar The Quarter Club offers a variety of quarters, sodas, mixer, shots and on the rock stuff which is definitely going to enthrall the guests. Less
